Frequently Asked Questions

What is SquibTurf?
SquibTurf is a social media app that allows users to share photos and videos with location-based content. Users can create "squibs" - short posts with media and captions.
How do I create a squib?
Tap the camera button to take a photo or record a video. Add a caption and post your squib to share with others in your area.
Why does the app need location access?
SquibTurf uses your location to show you content from people in your area and to tag your posts with location information.
How do I change my privacy settings?
Go to your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > SquibTurf to manage location permissions.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account directly from the app. Go to your Profile page (tap the gear icon), then tap "Delete Account" in the Settings section. You'll be asked to confirm your decision twice before the account is permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone and will remove all your data including your profile, posts, and account information.
I'm having trouble with the camera
Make sure you've granted camera permissions to SquibTurf in your device settings. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera > SquibTurf.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Tap the three dots on any squib and select "Report" to flag inappropriate content for review.
